About Me

The Sound of
Elegance

Welcome! I'm Manu, a professional saxophonist based in Dubai. With over a decade of international experience and established in the UAE since 2010, I bring soul-stirring melodies to life at some of the most prestigious venues in the region.

From intimate beach weddings to grand corporate galas, I craft musical experiences that captivate audiences and create lasting memories. I have performed as a resident saxophonist at iconic venues such as Cavalli Club, Scape at Burj Al Arab (now SALT), Nikki Beach, DRIFT Beach, Aura Skypool, Summersalt, and Level 43 Sky Lounge, and I have proudly been part of the entertainment at the Formula 1 Abu Dhabi for several years.
